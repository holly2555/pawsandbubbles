About us

At Paws and Bubbles, we are a friendly and professional dog grooming service based in Sallynoggin, dedicated to keeping your pets happy, healthy, and looking their very best. Founded in 2007, our team of experienced and passionate dog lovers provides a full range of grooming services, including bathing, clipping, styling, and nail care for dogs of all breeds and sizes. We take pride in creating a calm, safe, and stress-free environment, with a caring team that treats every dog like part of the family.
